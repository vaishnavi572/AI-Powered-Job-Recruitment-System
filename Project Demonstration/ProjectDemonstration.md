# Project Demonstration

# Introduction

The AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS) is a Salesforce-based application designed to simplify and automate the recruitment lifecycle. The demonstration highlights the system's major functionalities, automation, AI capabilities, and user interactions.

---

# Demonstration Objectives

- Demonstrate the complete recruitment workflow.
- Showcase Salesforce automation using Flows.
- Display AI-powered candidate evaluation.
- Demonstrate approval workflows.
- Present reports and dashboards.

---

# Demonstration Steps

## 1. Login to Salesforce

- Open Salesforce Developer Edition.
- Login with authorized credentials.
- Navigate to the AIRCMS Lightning App.

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview

Demonstrate:

- Recruitment Dashboard
- Open Job Positions
- Candidate Pipeline
- Hiring Statistics

---

## 3. Candidate Management

Demonstrate:

- Creating a new Candidate record.
- Updating candidate details.
- Viewing candidate information.

---

## 4. Job Opening Management

Demonstrate:

- Creating a Job Opening.
- Updating job details.
- Managing vacancies.
- Tracking job status.

---

## 5. Candidate Application

Demonstrate:

- Creating a Candidate Application.
- Linking Candidate with Job Opening.
- Updating application status.
- Viewing AI Candidate Score.

---

## 6. AI Features

Show:

- AI Candidate Summary
- AI Candidate Score
- Hiring Recommendations
- AI-generated insights

---

## 7. Automation

Demonstrate the Record-Triggered Flow.

Show:

- Candidate Score ≥ 85
- Automatic Email Notification
- Flow execution

---

## 8. Approval Process

Demonstrate:

- Salary Offer Submission
- Manager Approval
- Approval History
- Approval Notification

---

## 9. Reports & Dashboards

Display:

- Candidate Applications Report
- Recruitment Dashboard
- Hiring Progress
- Offer Approval Status

---

# Demonstration Outcome

The demonstration confirms that AIRCMS successfully automates recruitment activities, enhances hiring decisions using AI, improves workflow efficiency, and provides centralized recruitment management through Salesforce.
