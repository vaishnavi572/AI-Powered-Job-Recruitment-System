# Requirement Analysis

# Introduction

The AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS) is designed to simplify and automate the recruitment lifecycle using Salesforce CRM and Agentforce AI. The system enables recruiters and hiring managers to efficiently manage candidates, job openings, interviews, approvals, and onboarding through a centralized platform.

---

# Functional Requirements

## Candidate Management
- Create, update, and manage candidate profiles.
- Store candidate contact details, resumes, and application history.
- Track candidate status throughout the recruitment process.

## Job Opening Management
- Create and manage job openings.
- Define job descriptions, departments, salary ranges, and vacancies.
- Track job status (Open, Closed, On Hold).

## Candidate Application Management
- Link candidates to job openings.
- Record application dates and statuses.
- Maintain complete application history.

## AI Candidate Evaluation
- Generate AI-based candidate scores.
- Display AI-generated candidate summaries.
- Provide hiring recommendations and risk insights.

## Recruitment Workflow Automation
- Automate application processing using Salesforce Flows.
- Send notifications to recruiters.
- Update application statuses automatically.

## Interview Management
- Schedule interviews.
- Track interview rounds and feedback.
- Record interviewer comments.

## Offer Approval
- Submit salary offers for approval.
- Route approvals using Salesforce Approval Processes.
- Notify managers upon approval or rejection.

## Reporting and Dashboards
- Display recruitment metrics.
- Monitor hiring progress.
- Generate recruitment reports.

---

# Non-Functional Requirements

## Security
- Role-based access control.
- Secure authentication.
- Validation rules for data integrity.

## Performance
- Fast record retrieval.
- Efficient workflow execution.
- Real-time updates.

## Scalability
- Support high-volume recruitment.
- Allow future feature enhancements.

## Reliability
- Ensure system availability.
- Prevent data loss.

## Usability
- User-friendly Lightning interface.
- Simple navigation for recruiters and managers.
