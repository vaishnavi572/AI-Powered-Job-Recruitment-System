# Brainstorming and Ideation

## Overview

This phase focuses on identifying the recruitment challenges faced by organizations and exploring possible solutions using Salesforce CRM and Artificial Intelligence.

The goal was to design an intelligent recruitment platform capable of automating hiring workflows while improving candidate evaluation and recruitment efficiency.

## Documents Included

- Brainstorming.md
- Brainstorming_and_Ideation.pdf

## Outcome

After analyzing existing recruitment systems, the proposed solution was the **AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS)** built on Salesforce with Agentforce AI capabilities.
